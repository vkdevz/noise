About Noise Buds VS102 Neo

The Noise Buds VS102 Neo offer an unmatched audio experience with cutting-edge features like ENC with Quad Mic for crystal-clear calls and Hyper Sync for quick pairing. These earbuds boast a 40-hour battery life, and just 10 minutes of charging gives you 120 minutes of playtime thanks to Instacharge technology. With an 11mm speaker driver delivering immersive sound, and an IPX5 water-resistant rating, these buds are perfect for both everyday use and intense workouts. Enjoy seamless connectivity with Bluetooth 5.3 and experience the future of true wireless earbuds with the Noise Buds VS102 Neo.
